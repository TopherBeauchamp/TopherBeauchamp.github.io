About me

I'm a Computer Science student at Cal State University Dominguez Hills pursuing a BS with a minor in Physics, maintaining a 3.8 cumulative GPA. I serve as Vice President of our IEEE Branch where I've represented the university at conferences and organized campus presentations.

I'm currently working as a student researcher, which involves enhancing translation accuracy of healthcare papers by improving algorithmic approaches to neural machine translation and incorporating domain-specific knowledge. My Band

Me + You We're a punk band hailing from Redondo Beach, California, formed by drummer Topher Beauchamp, bassist & vocalist Nick Zavala, and guitarist David Lastiri. Later on, saxophonist Aiden Jennings and vocalist Kyle Rosenthal would join the group. Together, we have played 50+ shows, often using these events to raise funds for causes such as relief for Palisades fire victims and funds for no-kill animal shelters. We also ensure to maintain a wide variety of promoters, photographers, zine editors, and other artists/vendors in order to ensure the shows are as entertaining and community oriented as possible.

We've managed to obtain a following of 3,400 followers on Instagram, maintain 2,200 monthly listeners on Spotify, release an album completely mixed, mastered, and recorded in Nick's garage, and create a DIY merchandise line (David screen prints t-shirts & hoodies, I copy our music to CDr's & cassette tapes, Nick creates CD inlets / J cards for these). Working to combine my passion for both software engineering and music, I have been working on developing an online shop where jackets, shirts, CDs, etc can be purchased online.
