About me

My technical skills include Java, Swift, JavaScript, Python, and various web technologies like React, Node.js, and MongoDB, as well as experience with tools such as GitHub, Jira, and Confluence.

My Band

Me + You We're a punk band hailing from Redondo Beach, California, formed by drummer Topher Beauchamp, bassist & vocalist Nick Zavala, and guitarist David Lastiri. Later on, Saxophonist Aiden Jennings and vocalist Kyle Rosenthal would join the group. Together, we have played 50+ shows, often using these events to raise funds for causes such as relief for Palisades fire victims, funds for no-kill animal shelters, and more. We also ensure to maintain a wide variety of promoters, photographers, zine editors, and other artists such as painters and fashion designers in order to ensure the shows are as entertaining and community oriented as possible.
We've managed to obtain a following of just under 3,400 followers on Instagram, maintain 2,300 monthly listeners on Spotify, release an album completely mixed, mastered, and recorded in Nick's garage, and create a DIY merchandise line (David screen prints t-shirts & hoodies, I copy our music to CDr's & cassette tapes, Nick creates CD inlets / J cards for these).
